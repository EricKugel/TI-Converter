Instructions

  1. Download TI Connect CE from here on a Windows or Mac
  2. After installing, plug TI into computer
  3. Write or copy notes into the first box on the previous page, press convert, and copy everything in the output
  4. Open Connect CE and click the building blocks on the left side. Click New Program.
  5. Paste the output from the previous page and press save.
  6. Press the button of an arrow pointing towards a calculator.
  7. Select your calculator (if it doesn't show up, make sure it's on) and press send

To view the notes:

  1. On the calculator home screen, press PRGM.
  2. Select the program you just uploaded (it's probably at the top?) and press enter
  3. Press enter again and a page of notes will show. Enter any number and press enter to move to the next page.
